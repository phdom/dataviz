Strategic Measurement Framework

To support strategic alignment and continuous improvement, we've developed a set of short-term Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to track progress, focus efforts, and position RIPM for success.

These measures will evolve through our ongoing strategic review process. KPIs may be added, refined, or retired based on learning and changing priorities. Monthly strategy reviews will keep the strategic direction at the center of our process, allowing the management team to assess data, evaluate current initiatives, and adjust targets as needed.
